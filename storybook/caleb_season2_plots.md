# Caleb's Adventures — Season 2 Episode Plots (S02E01–S02E20)

## Characters
### Core Family
- **Caleb** — curious 3-year-old boy, light fair skin, hazel eyes, sandy-brown hair, red explorer vest, blue backpack with star keychain
- **Lily** — confident 5-year-old sister, sandy-brown braids with pink ribbons, denim overalls, hazel eyes
- **Bubbles** — golden retriever puppy, golden-cream fur, teal bandana with white paw prints
- **Sarah** (Mom) — sandy-brown ponytail, teal wrap top, gold locket, light freckles
- **Jack** (Dad) — sandy-brown hair, navy plaid flannel, pencil behind ear, light stubble
- **Nana Joy** (Grandmother) — silver-white bun, gold-rimmed glasses, burnt-orange cardigan

### Friends
- **Mila** — 3yo girl, black bob with red bow clip, mint-green smock dress, sketchbook
- **Aiden** — 4yo boy, dark brown skin, fade haircut, orange jersey #7, green sneakers
- **Sofia** — 3yo girl, tan-olive skin, curly hair with yellow headband, red polka-dot dress
- **Ravi** — 4yo boy, medium-brown skin, wavy black hair, blue-framed glasses, magnifying glass

### New in Season 2
- **Grandpa Joe** — Nana Joy's husband. Quiet, gentle, slightly hard of hearing. Silver hair, weathered hands, kind blue eyes behind thick round glasses. Forest-green vest over cream shirt, brown corduroy pants, worn leather boots. Always carries a silver harmonica in vest pocket.

---

## Episodes

### S02E01 — Caleb's First Day Jitters
- **Guests:** Mila, Aiden, Sofia, Ravi | **Scenes:** 12
- Caleb starts preschool and is too nervous to let go of Sarah's hand. Lily walks him to the door and gives him her lucky daisy pin. Inside, he discovers Mila, Aiden, Sofia, and Ravi are all in his class. By circle time, he's forgotten he was ever scared.
- **Moral:** Brave doesn't mean not scared — it means going anyway.

### S02E02 — The Snow Day Surprise
- **Guests:** — | **Scenes:** 12
- The first snowfall of the year. Caleb has never seen snow stick to the ground. Jack builds a lopsided snowman, Lily makes snow angels, and Bubbles keeps eating snowballs. Sarah makes hot cocoa for everyone, and Nana Joy knits tiny scarves for the snowman. Caleb names the snowman "Mr. Wobble."
- **Moral:** The best surprises come when you least expect them.

### S02E03 — Bubbles and the Baby Duckling
- **Guests:** — | **Scenes:** 12
- Bubbles finds a lost duckling in the backyard. Caleb and Lily try to reunite it with its family at the park pond. Bubbles is gentle and protective the whole way, carrying the duckling's leaf-boat. They find the mama duck and six siblings waiting.
- **Moral:** Being gentle with someone small is one of the biggest things you can do.

### S02E04 — Nana Joy's Mystery Box
- **Guests:** — | **Scenes:** 12
- Nana Joy brings a locked wooden box and says it was her mother's. She's lost the key. Caleb, Lily, and Bubbles search the cottage — under cushions, inside teapots, behind picture frames. They find the key hanging on a ribbon inside Nana Joy's old gardening hat. Inside the box: hand-drawn pictures Nana Joy made as a child.
- **Moral:** The most precious treasures are the ones made with love.

### S02E05 — Ravi's Bug Safari
- **Guests:** Ravi | **Scenes:** 13
- Ravi brings his magnifying glass and a homemade bug journal. He and Caleb catalogue every bug in the backyard — ants marching in lines, a ladybug on a leaf, a caterpillar on the fence. Bubbles accidentally destroys the ant highway and they have to rebuild it with tiny stick bridges.
- **Moral:** Every creature matters, no matter how small.

### S02E06 — The Farmer's Market Mix-Up
- **Guests:** — | **Scenes:** 12
- Sarah takes Caleb and Lily to the farmer's market. Caleb is in charge of the shopping list but can't read yet — he matches pictures to fruits. He grabs a dragon fruit thinking it's a "dragon egg," picks the biggest pumpkin they can barely carry, and trades his star keychain for a jar of honey (Jack trades it back).
- **Moral:** Helping out means trying, even when you're still learning.

### S02E07 — Sofia's Dance Recital
- **Guests:** Sofia | **Scenes:** 12
- Sofia is nervous about her first dance recital. Caleb and Lily make a giant "GO SOFIA" banner with glitter (it gets everywhere). At the recital, Sofia freezes on stage. Caleb stands up in the audience and does a silly wiggle dance to make her laugh — she starts dancing and doesn't stop.
- **Moral:** A true friend helps you find your courage.

### S02E08 — Jack's Fix-It Day
- **Guests:** — | **Scenes:** 12
- Jack decides to fix everything in the house in one day — the wobbly shelf, the squeaky door, the dripping faucet. Caleb is his "assistant" and hands him the wrong tools every time (a spatula instead of a screwdriver). Everything Jack fixes breaks again. Sarah quietly fixes them all after dinner.
- **Moral:** Asking for help doesn't mean you failed — it means you're smart.

### S02E09 — The Backyard Bird House
- **Guests:** Mila | **Scenes:** 13
- Mila and Caleb design a birdhouse. Mila paints it with polka dots and stripes. Caleb hammers a nail crooked. Lily adds a tiny welcome mat. They hang it in the tree and wait… and wait… and nothing. The next morning, Caleb wakes up to chirping — a wren family has moved in.
- **Moral:** Patience brings the sweetest rewards.

### S02E10 — Aiden's Soccer Showdown
- **Guests:** Aiden | **Scenes:** 12
- Aiden teaches Caleb to play soccer. Caleb kicks the ball backward, trips over it, and accidentally scores on his own goal. Bubbles steals the ball three times. They make up their own rules: "puppy goals count double" and "falling down means you get a hug." The final score is 47 to 52 and nobody knows who won.
- **Moral:** Making your own rules can be more fun than winning.

### S02E11 — The Lost Star Keychain
- **Guests:** — | **Scenes:** 12
- Caleb loses his favorite star keychain from his blue backpack. He retraces his steps — the kitchen, the garden, the park, Nana Joy's cottage. Everyone helps look. Bubbles finds it buried in his own food bowl. Caleb hugs Bubbles and decides to attach it more tightly.
- **Moral:** Sometimes what you're looking for is closer than you think.

### S02E12 — The Neighborhood Talent Show
- **Guests:** Mila, Aiden, Sofia, Ravi | **Scenes:** 14
- The kids put on a talent show in the backyard. Mila does speed painting, Aiden does soccer tricks, Sofia dances, and Ravi performs "magic" science experiments (baking soda volcano). Caleb can't decide what his talent is. He ends up being the announcer — and his funny introductions steal the show.
- **Moral:** Your talent might be something you never expected.

### S02E13 — Grandpa Joe's Visit
- **Guests:** — | **Scenes:** 13
- Nana Joy's husband Grandpa Joe visits for the first time. He's quiet and a little hard of hearing. Caleb thinks Grandpa Joe doesn't like him because he doesn't talk much. But then Grandpa Joe pulls out a harmonica and plays the most beautiful music. They spend the afternoon making music together — Caleb on pots and pans, Grandpa Joe on harmonica.
- **Moral:** People show love in different ways — sometimes you just have to listen.

### S02E14 — The Puddle Jumping Championship
- **Guests:** Aiden | **Scenes:** 12
- After a big rainstorm, Caleb and Aiden compete to find the biggest puddle. They rate each puddle on "splashiness." Bubbles wins by belly-flopping into a mud puddle and soaking everyone. Sarah is not thrilled when they come home covered in mud, but Jack secretly gives them points for "maximum splash."
- **Moral:** Getting messy is part of the fun.

### S02E15 — Lily's Secret Project
- **Guests:** — | **Scenes:** 12
- Lily keeps disappearing into her room and won't let Caleb in. He thinks she's mad at him. He tries to apologize for things he didn't do. He bakes her a sorry-cookie (it's burnt). Finally Lily reveals: she was making a scrapbook of all their adventures together as a birthday surprise for him.
- **Moral:** Not every secret is a bad one — some are made with love.

### S02E16 — The Tricycle Adventure
- **Guests:** — | **Scenes:** 12
- Caleb tries to ride Lily's old tricycle. He wobbles, crashes into the flower bed, rolls into a bush, and gets his vest caught on the handlebar. Jack runs alongside spotting him. After twenty tries, Caleb pedals three whole meters without falling. The whole family cheers like he won the Olympics.
- **Moral:** Every big journey starts with one wobbly pedal.

### S02E17 — The Treasure Map Part 2
- **Guests:** Ravi | **Scenes:** 13
- Caleb finds a second treasure map in the backyard — but this one is in Nana Joy's handwriting and looks old. He and Ravi follow the clues: "where the sun says goodnight" (the west fence), "where music grows" (the wind chime tree), "where love is planted" (Sarah's garden). The treasure is a tin box of letters Nana Joy wrote to her grandchildren before they were born.
- **Moral:** Some treasures have been waiting for you your whole life.

### S02E18 — The Puppet Show
- **Guests:** Sofia, Mila | **Scenes:** 13
- A rainy day sends everyone inside. Caleb, Mila, and Sofia make sock puppets and put on a play. The plot makes no sense — a dragon who wants to be a baker, a princess who fixes cars, and a puppy superhero (Bubbles in a cape makes a cameo). Jack films it. Nana Joy laughs until she cries.
- **Moral:** The silliest stories are sometimes the ones people remember most.

### S02E19 — The Fire Station Visit
- **Guests:** Mila, Aiden, Sofia, Ravi | **Scenes:** 13
- The kids visit the neighborhood fire station. Caleb sits in the fire truck and accidentally honks the horn (scaring everyone). Aiden tries on a helmet that covers his entire head. Sofia asks if they rescue cats from trees (they do). Ravi asks about water pressure (of course). Bubbles gets an honorary fire-dog badge.
- **Moral:** Heroes come in all shapes and help in all kinds of ways.

### S02E20 — Caleb's Birthday Wish (Season Finale)
- **Guests:** Mila, Aiden, Sofia, Ravi | **Scenes:** 14
- Caleb turns 4. Sarah bakes a caterpillar cake (it looks more like a snake). Jack builds a piñata that won't break no matter how hard they swing. Nana Joy and Grandpa Joe bring a hand-carved wooden toolbox "for a big boy." All the friends come. When it's time to blow out candles, Caleb closes his eyes for a long time. Lily asks what he wished for. Caleb says, "Nothing. I already have everything."
- **Moral:** The richest person is the one who knows what they already have.

## Season 2 Themes
Growing up, starting school, trying new things, patience, family legacy, creativity, self-discovery, appreciating what you have.
